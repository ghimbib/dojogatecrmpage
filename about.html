About DojoGate

Last Updated: April 9, 2026

DojoGate is built for B2B teams that lose time, margin, and momentum in approval chains, spreadsheet pricing, and messy follow-up. We focus on one thing: helping teams quote faster and close cleaner.

What We Build

DojoGate combines pipeline visibility, quote workflows, pricing guardrails, and approval routing in one system. The goal is simple: fewer stalled deals, faster approvals, and fewer margin-killing mistakes.

Who It’s For

  • Hardware and distribution teams with complex pricing and longer sales cycles
  • Service businesses that need approvals, quote controls, and cleaner handoffs
  • Operators who are tired of running revenue through spreadsheets and inboxes
Practical by design. DojoGate is not trying to be a generic CRM with a pile of add-ons. It is built around real quote, pricing, approval, and follow-up work.
